Professional Project Management

We fully recognize the fact that beginning and monitoring a home construction project can be very overwhelming. Therefore, to eliminate this concern, LaBaugh Builders manages every phase of construction in accordance to explicit and thorough guidelines.

Beginning with our initial meeting, we will be there to guide you through all the stages of design, planning, obtaining permits, construction, inspections, as well as the final, concluding details. A monthly budget will be provided along with scheduled reports tracking the process of your project. This service offers you the time you need to make important decisions while also providing you the assurance that your project is being completed in accordance with the contract.
